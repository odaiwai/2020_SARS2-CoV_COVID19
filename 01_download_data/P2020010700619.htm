Suspected case tests negative for MERS-CoV
******************************************
     The Centre for Health Protection of the Department of Health today (January 7) reported that the suspected case of Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS) pending results yesterday (January 6), upon preliminary testing, tested negative for MERS Coronavirus.
